How to Meditate (Simple Steps)

  1. Find a comfortable seat (chair or cushion).
  2. Set a short time (3–5 minutes is great for beginners).
  3. Gently close your eyes or soften your gaze.
  4. Breathe naturally and notice the breath in and out.
  5. When your mind wanders, kindly return to the breath.

Tip: Start small and be consistent.
